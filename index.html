    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers about our 3D printed fossils and ordering process

    Are the fossils real?

    No — our fossils are 3D printed replicas made from high-quality resin and hand-painted for a realistic look.

    Do you ship internationally?

    Yes, we ship worldwide! Delivery times vary depending on your region.

    Can I request a custom fossil?

    Absolutely! Send us a message with the fossil or species you want, and we’ll design it for you.

    What materials do you use?

    We use eco-friendly PLA or resin filament, depending on the detail level of each model.
